STEPHEN ALBERTS

Generative art, sculpture, painting, and animation have been my focus over the past year. Using algorithms in my work has been an exciting discovery for me in the digital space. There is a conversation and blending of ideas between my digital and physical work.

I will be finishing my BFA at the University of Minnesota in May 2022. I have an exhibition at the Nash Gallery along with the other graduating BFA students on May 3-14, 2022.
